Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I sync my clipboard items across devices?

PasteKit uses iCloud to sync your clipboard items automatically across all your Apple devices. To enable sync:

  • iOS/iPadOS: Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Make sure PasteKit is enabled
  • macOS: System Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Make sure PasteKit is enabled

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices.

Q: Why does the keyboard need "Allow Full Access"?

The keyboard extension needs "Allow Full Access" to:

  • Read clipboard content when you add new items
  • Access your saved clipboard items
  • Open the main PasteKit app from the keyboard

Important: We do NOT track your typing, record keystrokes, or send any data over the network. All your data stays in your personal iCloud account.

Q: How do I set up the keyboard on iOS?
  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Keyboard ‚Üí Keyboards
  2. Tap Add New Keyboard
  3. Select PasteKit from the list
  4. Tap on PasteKit and enable Allow Full Access
  5. When typing, tap the üåê icon to switch to PasteKit keyboard
Q: How do I organize my clipboard items?

You can organize clipboard items using tags:

  • When creating or editing an item, add tags separated by commas (e.g., "work, email, important")
  • Use the Tags tab to view items filtered by tag
  • Search across content, notes, and tags using the search bar
Q: Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your data is completely private:

  • All data is stored in YOUR personal iCloud account
  • Data is encrypted using Apple's iCloud encryption
  • We never see, access, or store your data on our servers (we don't have any servers)
  • Your data never leaves Apple's ecosystem

See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Q: How do I export my data?

On macOS:

  • Click the menu icon (three dots) in the toolbar
  • Select "Export Data to Files"
  • Choose a location to save your data as JSON

On iOS: Export is currently available on macOS only.

Q: How do I delete my data?

To delete individual items:

  • Swipe left on any item and tap Delete (iOS)
  • Right-click and select Delete (macOS)

To delete all data:

  • Delete the PasteKit app from your device
  • Disable iCloud sync for PasteKit in Settings
Q: PasteKit won't sync. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices
  2. Check that iCloud is enabled for PasteKit in Settings/System Settings
  3. Ensure you have an active internet connection
  4. Try manually syncing: Tap the sync button in the app menu
  5. Restart the app and wait a few minutes for sync to complete

If problems persist, contact us at pastekit1@gmail.com

Q: Can I use PasteKit without iCloud?

Yes! PasteKit works without iCloud sync. Your data will be stored locally on your device only. To disable iCloud sync:

  • iOS: Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Toggle off PasteKit
  • macOS: System Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Toggle off PasteKit
